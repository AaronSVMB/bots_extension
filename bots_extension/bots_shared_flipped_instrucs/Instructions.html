Conversion Rate

During the experiment, your earnings will be measured in points. Each point is worth 2 cents, so at the end of the experiment your points will be converted to dollars at a rate of 50 points per dollar.


Your Groups

  • You will participate in two groups simultaneously
    • The Green group
    • The Blue group
  • Each group will consist of 4 members (including you), and you will be in these groups for {{ C.NUM_PERIODS }} periods
  • Note â€“ All three participants in your Green group are different from the three participants in your Blue group
  • You will not know the identity or the individual decisions of the participants you are grouped with


The AI participants

  • Some of the participants in this experiment, members of your Green and your Blue group, are computerized AI's (artificial intelligence)
    • No group in this experiment has more than 2 AI players
  • All other participants are human participants and are currently in this room


Your Decision Screen

  • To the right is the decision screen that you will see in each period
  • The current period is displayed in the top right (shown in purple)
  • At the start of each period, you will receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} (shown in orange)


Making Your Decision

  • In each period, you must decide how many of your {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} to invest to your
    • Green Group's Joint Account (shown in Green)
    • Blue Group's Joint Account (shown in Blue)
    • Personal Account
  • To invest points to your Green Group's Joint Account, enter a whole number between 0 and {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} in the Blue circled region
  • To invest points to your Blue Group's Joint Account, enter a whole number between 0 and {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} in the Green circled region
  • The computer automatically invests the rest of your points in your Personal Account

Example

  • Suppose you
    • Invest 3 points to your Green Group's Joint Account
    • Invest 6 points to your Blue Group's Joint Account
    • Then, by clicking the next button, the computer will automatically invest the rest of your points, 11, into your Personal Account (20 - 3 - 6 = 11).


Important Note

  • Your investment across your Green and Blue Group's Joint Accounts in each period cannot exceed the {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} you receive each period
  • For example,
    • The investment of 15 points to your Green Group's Joint Account and 12 points to your Blue Group's Joint Account, as seen in the picture to the right, is an infeasible combination as their sum exceeds the {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} you receive each period.


AI Participant Decision

  • The AI's are programmed with algorithms that guide how they invest in each period
  • The algorithms are designed to imitate the behavior of different human participants who have participated in similar experiments earlier this semester
  • Each AI faces the same decision as you
    • They each decide how many of their {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} to invest in their Green Group, Blue Group, and Personal Account
    • Like you, each AI makes their own decision without knowing the individual decisions or identities of the other players in their groups


Period Earnings

  • Your earnings in a period are the sum of your earnings from
    • Green Group's Joint Account (your Green Individual Share)
    • Blue Group's Joint Account (your Blue Individual Share)
    • Personal Account

Group's Joint Accounts

  • Earnings from your Green and Blue Group's Joint Accounts
    • Depends on the total number of points that you and other members of the group invest in the Joint Account
    • Each member will earn 0.5 times the total number of points invested in the Group's Joint Account
Group Earnings = ( income from Group's Joint Account [= Your Individual Share] )
Which is the same as
= ( 0.5 x sum of all investments to the Group's Joint Account )

Personal Account

The points you place in your Personal Account will be directly added to your earnings. (For Example, if you place 5 points in your Personal Account then you will earn 5 points from your Personal Account.)

Your Personal Account (in one period) is determined as follows:

Personal Account = ( 20 ) - ( your investment to your Green Group's Joint Account ) - ( your investment to your Blue Group's Joint Account )

Total Earnings

Total Earnings (in one period) = ( Personal Account ) + ( Green Individual Share ) + (Blue Individual Share )

Which is the same as
= ( 20 ) - ( your investment to your Green Group's Joint Account ) - ( your investment to your Blue Group's Joint Account )
+ ( 0.5 x sum of all investments to the Green Group's Joint Account )
+ ( 0.5 x sum of all investments to the Blue Group's Joint Account )

Example

Investments

  • Suppose you invest
    • 7 points to your Green Group's Joint Account
    • 5 points to your Blue Group's Joint Account
  • And the three other members of your
    • Green Group invest a total of 23 points
    • Blue Group invest a total of 17 points
Earnings
  • Group Earnings: (Your Investment + Total of the three Other Members' Investments) x (0.5)
    • Green: (7 + 23) x (0.5) = 30 x 0.5 = 15 points (Green Individual Share)
    • Blue: (5 + 17) x (0.5) = 22 x 0.5 = 11 points (Blue Individual Share)
  • Personal Account: (points received) - (your Green investment) - (your Blue investment)
    • (20) - (7) - (5) = 8 points
  • Total Earnings: (Green Individual Share) + (Blue Individual Share)+ (Personal Account)
    • (15) + (11) + (8) = 34 points
